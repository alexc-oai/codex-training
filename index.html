What Codex Can and Cannot Do

This is the mental model to carry into every task.

Can do well

  • Ask, plan, scaffold, and summarize scoped work quickly.
  • Draft strong first-pass outputs across code, apps, briefs, and handoffs.

Cannot do reliably

  • Infer context you did not provide.
  • Guarantee correctness without tests and review.

Always verify: run checks, validate assumptions, and review outputs before sharing.
